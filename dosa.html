ABOUT
DOSA
Dr. Inderveer Chana

TICC offers confidential, free professional mental health services to student’s at TIET. Our goal is to assist students as they adjust to university life, cope with stress, and take full advantage of TIET’s academic and social opportunities. At TICC, students develop strategies to manage personal problems and situational crises. They develop self-awareness, resilience, and skills for lifelong-learning.

Dr. SONAM DULLAT

I believe healing comes after we accept ourselves and our life the way it is. There is no such thing as an easy life, everyone has a story and fair share of struggles. What we need is more open conversations, to share our story, so that we can free ourselves from our pain, anger and shortcomings.

Psychological well-being, by which I also mean emotional and behavioural competency, is something I believe as important as our physical well-being and requires an equal amount of effort to keep it sound. Resolving the inner conflicts by understanding one’s own self and experiencing a sense of freedom is what psychotherapy sets its sight on.
